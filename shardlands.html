The Shardlands are a fractured expanse of crystalline terrain, remnants of a cataclysmic weave rupture. Magic here is volatile, memory unreliable, and fate tangled. Travelers speak of echoing voices, floating shards, and time slipping sideways.

“The Loom frayed here first.”

Thalurin (Capital)

Placement: At the southern base of the Islarn Peaks, where the mountain road descends toward the Riverflow plains.

Description: The largest forge-city in the Shardlands, carved directly into the mountain’s flank. The forges never cool, and the clang of hammers is a constant hymn.

Stoneveil

Placement: Southwest of Thalurin, on a high escarpment overlooking the valley.

Description: A fortified mining town that refines ore before shipping it to Thalurin. Known for its wind-carved stone arches.

Grimhholt

Placement: Directly south of Thalurin, guarding the primary pass to the Riverflow Basin.

Description: A walled trade town with sturdy, cavernous streets. Every caravan bound for Calvendir passes through its gates.

Frostmere

Placement: Northeast of Thalurin, nestled in a cold valley along a tributary stream.

Description: A hardy community of hunters, tanners, and brewers who specialize in smoked meats and ice-fermented spirits.

Keldran’s Rest

Placement: South-southwest of Thalurin, in a fertile bowl between two ridges.

Description: A blackstone village extracting high-grade iron ore used in Thalurin’s forges. Despite its somber name, it’s very much alive and thriving.
