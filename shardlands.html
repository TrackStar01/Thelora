The Shardlands are the fractured heart of Thelora — a land of fertile plains, splintered mountains, and lingering scars of wild magic. Once shattered by the Breakings, its people now cling to trade, faith, and stubborn survival amid the echoes of a wounded Weave.

“The Loom frayed here first.”

Geological Map of the Shardlands

Thalruin (Capital)

Placement: At the southern base of the Islarn Peaks, where the mountain road descends toward the Riverflow plains.

Description: The largest forge-city in the Shardlands, carved directly into the mountain’s flank. The forges never cool, and the clang of hammers is a constant hymn.

Stoneveil

Placement: Southwest of Thalruin, on a high escarpment overlooking the valley.

Description: A fortified mining town that refines ore before shipping it to Thalruin. Known for its wind-carved stone arches.

Grimhholt

Placement: Directly south of Thalruin, guarding the primary pass to the Riverflow Basin.

Description: A walled trade town with sturdy, cavernous streets. Every caravan bound for Calvendir passes through its gates.

Frostmere

Placement: Northeast of Thalruin, nestled in a cold valley along a tributary stream.

Description: A hardy community of hunters, tanners, and brewers who specialize in smoked meats and ice-fermented spirits.

Keldran’s Rest

Placement: South-southwest of Thalruin, in a fertile bowl between two ridges.

Description: A blackstone village extracting high-grade iron ore used in Thalruin’s forges. Despite its somber name, it’s very much alive and thriving.

Calvendir (Capital)

Placement: Central river confluence.

Description: Capital of the Compact. A bustling trade city where river routes converge, known for its diplomacy and grain markets.

Esterbrook

Placement: Northeast, across the bridge.

Description: Grain and milling hub. Its windmills and granaries feed half the Compact’s frontier.

Tallowreach

Placement: Southeast, river bogs.

Description: A Fenlight village steeped in mist and mystery. Known for its herbalists and whispering marshes.

Lowhollow

Placement: South of the Fraying Fields, upland plain.

Description: A dryland farming and frontier trade post. Its caravans are the lifeblood of the southern Compact.

Ashenfield

Placement: South of the crater.

Description: Reborn farmlands nourished by volcanic soil. Once scorched, now thriving with resilient crops and hardy settlers.

Redmarch

Placement: Northwest of the crater.

Description: Western trade crossroads. Its crimson banners mark the edge of Compact influence and the start of contested lands.

Varnmir (Capital)

Placement: Eastern plains, forest border.

Description: Trade and druidic governance center guided by the rhythm of the Weavewild.

Greymantle

Placement: North across the bridge.

Description: Stonecutting and caravan hub in hilly uplands.

Fellcross

Placement: Far north.

Description: Cold frontier watchpost guarding high passes.

Brindlecross

Placement: South in wooded lowlands.

Description: Herbal trade center known for rootcraft and diplomacy.

Wynmere

Placement: South-southeast near the Weavewild.

Description: Lake town famed for potion trade and moonlit rituals.

Merrowfen (Capital)

Placement: South of the central mountains.

Description: A sandstone trade city built on descending terraces and cooled by deep wells. Its markets stretch across shaded courtyards, and its caravans reach every corner of the Shardlands.

Sunhold

Placement: Westernmost of the southern plains towns.

Description: Beacon-topped town whose bronze-roofed towers reflect the sunrise. Acts as the League’s eastern agricultural hub and road watchpoint.

Driftheaven

Placement: Central southern plains.

Description: Windmill town and caravan rest stop amid vast grasslands. Known for its horse herds and midsummer Great Drift Festival.

Velin’s Gate

Placement: Easternmost of the southern plains towns.

Description: Fortified border town guarding the western trade routes. Its echoing watchtowers guide travelers through the passes at dusk.

Saltmere

Placement: North of Merrowfen’s plains, south of Brindlecross.

Description: Border trade town where forest herbs and timber meet salt and leather from the south. Called “the scent line” for the mingling smells of pine and dust.
