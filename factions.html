Factions & Orders

The Threadwalkers

Nomadic seers who traverse the Loom’s pathways, interpreting echoes of memory and prophecy. Their robes shimmer with woven glyphs, and their presence often precedes great change.

The Loombinders

Scholars and artificers who attempt to anchor the Loom’s power into relics, architecture, and rituals. Their sanctums are hidden within ley-threaded ruins.

The Unraveled

A fractured cult who believe the Loom must be undone to restore free will. Their threadmarks pulse with unstable energy, and their actions threaten Theoria’s balance.
