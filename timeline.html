Timeline of Theoria

Pre-Weave Era

Before the Loom’s awakening, Theoria was fragmented—its regions isolated, its magic wild and untethered. Ancient ruins hint at proto-thread rituals and forgotten gods.

The Loom’s Emergence

Scholars mark the First Pulse as the moment the Loom began binding memory to matter. Threadmarks appeared, and prophecy became a tangible force.

The Weaveborn Awakening

Your campaign begins here. The party awakens in the Grove of Four Seasons, each bearing a threadmark. The Loom pulses unnaturally, and regions begin to stir.

Current Cycle

  • Autumn’s Veil: Threadwalkers report distortions in the Loom’s rhythm.
  • Emberfall: A buried temple rises in Vael’Ashai.
  • Frostwake: A prophecy shard fractures in the Shardlands.
