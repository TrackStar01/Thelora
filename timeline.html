Pre-Ages (Timeless)

The Creator weaves the Weave and sets the first threads of reality. This era exists outside of time, forming the mythic foundation of existence.

First Age (1A) — The Sundering of Stars

  • 0 1A: A cosmic war among the gods. The Dark One attempts to unravel the Weave directly.
  • Stars were said to have "bled" into the void.
  • Ancient civilizations were erased, entire continents shattered.
  • Only the intervention of the Creator’s loyal gods saved what little remained.
  • ~1500 1A: Mortal survivors begin to rebuild fragments of civilization.

Second Age (2A) — The Shattered Age

  • 0 2A: Grand mortal empires rise from the ashes of the First Age.
  • The Gods, weakend from the war and shattering have little to no interaction with the material plane.
  • Powerful spellcasters are left practically unchecked.
  • Mortals, drunk on stolen knowledge of the Weave, rose up in arrogance.
  • Attempts made to forge their own gods and Weave constructs destabilized reality.
  • ~2000 2A: Magical overreach leads to instability. Plagues, storms, and mutations devastate the world.
  • Powerful artifacts from this time still lurk, often unstable and dangerous.

Third Age (3A) — The Rise and Fall of the Tyrant King

  • 0 3A: Scattered kingdoms emerge from the ruins.
  • 198 3A: Kaelen Vorr is born.
  • 400 3A: A man marked as a Champion of the Weave, Kaelen Vorr, is born.
  • 430 3A: Kaelen is manipulated by the Dark One
  • 435 3A: Kaelen’s fall begins—despair and hubris take root.
  • 450 3A: Kaelen becomes the Tyrant King.
  • 470 3A: Kaelen breaks free of the Dark One's grasp and sacrifices himself to forge the Great Seal, ending the Third Age.

Fourth Age (4A) — The Present Era

  • 0 4A: The Era of Restoration begins.
  • 150 4A: First disturbances in the Weave; ancient relics stir.
  • 250 4A: Zealot groups rise; magic suppression spreads.
  • 300 4A (Present): The Seal visibly weakens. Prophecies awaken. The Weaveborn rise.
